Welcome to TRNG Web Service!

We are serving True Random Numbers to anyone on the internet.

You might have pondered how machines, which are inherently deterministic, can produce randomness. In fact, most random numbers utilized in computer programs are pseudo-random. This means they are produced in a predictable manner using a mathematical formula. While this is adequate for a wide range of applications, it may not provide the randomness you anticipate if you’re accustomed to the unpredictability of dice rolls or lottery drawings.

Unlike pseudo-random number generators that use mathematical formulas, the TRNG web service generates randomness from a fundamentally unpredictable physical process - thermal noise. Thermal noise, also known as Johnson-Nyquist noise, is the electronic noise generated by the thermal agitation of the charge carriers inside an electrical conductor. This noise is a powerful source of randomness because it’s inherently unpredictable and cannot be reliably reproduced. This makes the TRNG web service an excellent choice when you need true randomness in your applications.
