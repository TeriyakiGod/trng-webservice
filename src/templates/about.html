About

TRNG Web Service employs advanced algorithms and hardware to generate truly unpredictable numbers, crucial for cryptography, simulations, and gaming applications. With its robust architecture, it ensures high-quality random numbers that pass stringent statistical tests for randomness. Users can access this service via an intuitive web interface or integrate it seamlessly into their applications through APIs.

For my Engineering Thesis, I delved deep into the intricate workings of random number generation, exploring various methodologies and algorithms. Developing TRNG Web Service was not only a challenging technical endeavor but also a rewarding experience, as it bridges the theoretical foundations of randomness with practical applications. Through meticulous research and rigorous testing, I aimed to create a reliable tool that contributes to the security and efficiency of diverse technological domains. TRNG Web Service stands as a testament to my dedication to advancing the field of random number generation and its pivotal role in modern computing.
